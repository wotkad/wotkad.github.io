Cookie policy

Cookies are small size files sent by the website, which are saved on website visitors device. Cookies help website to identity the visitor and keep track of his interactions for later use, i.e. setting language preference. Cookies are used in most websites, in detail this mechanic is defined in RFC6265.

Vide Infra uses cookies to optimize our websites and improve our user experience, by analyzing website visitor interactions using 3rd party solutions. For this analysis Google inc. tool Google Analyitics and Yandex LLC tool Yandex Metrica are used, you can learn more about those solutions privacy policies and terms of use in their respective webpages: Google Analytics privacy and terms, Yandex Metrica privacy and terms. Opt-out from 3rd party cookies is possible with those plugins: Google Analytics and Yandex Metrica.
